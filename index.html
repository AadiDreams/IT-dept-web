Welcome to QubIT

Join our college's premier IT association to explore innovative projects, attend engaging tech events, and collaborate with fellow technology enthusiasts. Discover the power of community and cutting-edge technology with QubIT.

Association of IT Department

QubIT is the official IT association of College of Engineering Thalassery, composed of passionate students, faculty, and industry experts. We organize events, workshops, and seminars that cover a wide range of IT topics, from software development to cybersecurity.

About QubIT

Mission

Our mission at QubIT is to foster a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, providing opportunities for students to explore, innovate, and excel in information technology.

Vision

We envision a collaborative environment where students can develop technical skills, engage in innovative projects, and prepare for successful careers in the IT industry.

Values

At QubIT, we value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to create an inclusive community that encourages curiosity and excellence in all aspects of information technology.
